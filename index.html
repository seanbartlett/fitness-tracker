Progression Rule

Complete all 3 sets at the top of the rep range with clean form → add 5 lbs. You'll drop back to 8–9 reps. Work back up. When you max 50 lbs, switch to slow tempo (3 sec down) or add band resistance.

12-Month Phases
Months 1–3 · Foundation
Build the Habits

Track food even badly. Hit 3 lifts/week 80% of the time. Don't optimize — just show up. Expected: 12–18 lbs lost.

Months 4–6 · Momentum
Tighten the System

Review tracking data. Find calorie leaks (weekends, restaurants). Add a 10-min after-dinner walk. Expected cumulative: 18–24 lbs.

Months 7–12 · Execution
Close It Out

Don't cut calories further — increase training volume slightly. Add third cardio session when life allows. Stay aggressive on protein. Expected cumulative: 42–60 lbs.
